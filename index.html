Suzuki Method

Musical ability can be developed in all children.

Early Start

About Mary Mathews

  • Music Teachers National Association Member
  • Suzuki Trained
  • Founding teacher of the Royal Conservatory Music Development Program
  • 15+ years teaching experience

Hi, I'm Mary!

When I was 8 years old, I took my first classical piano lesson, and my life was forever changed.

In 2010 I became a full time piano teacher, devoting my life to instilling a love of music in each child I teach.

In between, I worked in television production by day, and played in bands by night. All throughout, I've kept music in my life, and I hope you and your children will too.

Whether you're a musician wanting to pass that down to your children, or have never played music yourself, but want your child to benefit from a life of music, I'd love to meet you and your child and pass along the gifts I've been given by my teachers.
